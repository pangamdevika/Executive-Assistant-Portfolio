About

Devika is a dynamic, results-oriented executive partner with over eighteen years of cross-industry experience supporting CXOs, Managing Directors and Promoter groups. Her work sits at the intersection of governance, operations and strategy — anticipating what leadership needs before it's asked for.

She has built a reputation as a trusted strategic partner inside management teams: streamlining executive workflows, coordinating HR and vendor relationships, and holding the discretion required to operate at the Founder's Office level. A proactive approach paired with sharp interpersonal instinct keeps stakeholders aligned even inside fast-moving, high-stakes environments.

Her range spans hospitality, legal and consulting practices, manufacturing, private equity and the cultural sector — proof that the discipline of running an executive office translates cleanly across very different worlds.
